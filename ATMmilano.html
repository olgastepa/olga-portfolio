Mobile app for ATM Milano

  • UX
  • UI
  • Mobile app design
  • New features design

The goal

Redesign a mobile application for the transport company ATM Milano, Metro in Milan.

News

Browse news by category: hang-ups, technical problems, opening of new stations.

Online route

Selection of the required metro stations. The screen shows where to change trains, how many stops to travel.

Buying a ticket

Selecting the desired ticket, purchasing additional options.

Payment method

Choice of payment method: SMS, Apple Pay, PayPal, bank card.

ОOnline ticket

QR code and the ability to enter / register in the application to remember the purchased ticket.

Apple Pay

Function of payment for services using Apple Pay. Payment within the application using touch ID.

Online one-time ticket

QR code function, view the number of minutes remaining.

Creating a personal account

The function of registering a personal account in the application.

Personal account

Possibility of choosing the desired function: buying an online travel pass, a one-time ticket, replenishing the balance of a travel pass, replenishing an ATM account.

Online travel card

The ability to use an online pass, see the expiration date, buy a pass online.
