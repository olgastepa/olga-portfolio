The goal

Design some pages for The Trumpet.

The Trumpet

Colorful color palette, elegant and trendy fonts, images from the fashion world, unique UI design.

Main page

A colorful, yet simple and sophisticated image. Calm but big headline. Monochrome menu bar. All this gives the client an idea of ​​what the website is talking about and what services it provides.

Catwalks page

A big headline, a large-scale image, top trends - everything is positioned to best capture the attention of customers.

Editorial page

The page is divided into sections: interviews, quotes, recommendations.

Fashion Stories Page

News of the world of fashion. Latest trends. Lightweight design filled with free space for better visualization of products.

News page

Fashion news is divided into categories for easy search and display of information.

News page

Each section has an action button, which makes finding information as easy as possible.

Fashion Shows Page

Attention is paid to a separate brand, large image size.
